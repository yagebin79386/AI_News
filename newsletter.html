Inside Google’s Two-Year Frenzy to Catch Up With OpenAI

2025-03-21

In the competitive landscape of AI, Google is striving to monetize its Gemini app amidst a backdrop of intense competition and financial pressures. Despite having fewer app installs compared to OpenAI's ChatGPT, Google is exploring ad-based revenue models while grappling with potential antitrust revenue losses. The tech giant is also racing to develop advanced AI capabilities, such as agentic AI, to keep pace with rivals. As the industry invests heavily in generative AI, the challenge remains to achieve profitability and sustainability, with Google executives emphasizing cautious advancement to avoid past missteps.

Are we close to an intelligence explosion?

2025-03-21

The concept of an intelligence explosion, where AI surpasses human intelligence and enters a phase of rapid self-improvement, is gaining traction among tech leaders and researchers. Figures like Sam Altman of OpenAI and pioneers Geoffrey Hinton and Yoshua Bengio suggest that superintelligence could be just a few years away. This potential leap in AI capabilities raises both hopes and fears: while it could revolutionize fields like medicine and climate science, it also poses existential risks if not properly controlled. The debate continues on whether this future is imminent and how society should prepare for such transformative technological advancements.

TOP NEWS!

Yahoo Is Still Here—and It Has Big Plans for AI

2025-03-21

For the top news of this edition, Yahoo has big plans for AI under the leadership of CEO Jim Lanzone. Despite Yahoo's past blunders and missed opportunities, Lanzone sees the company as an unacknowledged gem with billions in revenue. He has focused on improving the company's existing offerings, cutting losses, making strategic acquisitions, and boosting profits. With a strong emphasis on artificial intelligence, Yahoo has made strategic moves like acquiring Artifact, an AI-powered news aggregator, to enhance its offerings. Yahoo's renewed focus on AI and its quick turnaround since Lanzone took over show that the company still has a lot to offer in the tech industry.
